Operational Environment

Throughout South Africa, almost all rural and urban settlements are challenged with limited water for people, industry and agriculture. It became increasingly evident over the last couple of years that water shortages will occur increasingly. In this complex environment, some driving factors can be identified as:

  • Increased usage due to the increasing population
  • Deterioration of the quality of water sources due to pollution and over usage
  • Changing weather patterns
  • Non-revenue earning water
  • Water / Sanitation relationship
  • Ageing infrastructure
  • Operations and maintenance challenges
